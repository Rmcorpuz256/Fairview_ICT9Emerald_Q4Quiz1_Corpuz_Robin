First Quarter Project:


This is my first quarter project, it revolves around the topic "Noli me Tangere", which is a book written by Jose rizal. My project mainly gives descriptions to some of the characters in the book such as Crisostomo Ibarra, Maria Clara, Crispin, Baslio, Sisa and ect.



Characters in Noli me Tangere
Picture Name Description
Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Crisostomo ibarra is a respectful and refined man, he recently had returned back to the Philippines after studying in europe for 7 years.
  • He is a very wealthy man being the son of Don Rafael.
  • He becomes vengeful because he found out what had happened to his father Don Rafael.
Maria Clara.
  • She is the daughter of Kapitan Tiago, however her biological father is Padre Damaso.
  • She is a respected woman with refined manners who is the fiancee of Crisostomo Ibarra.
Padre Damaso
  • Padre Damaso is a ruthless and bad priest who spent his lifetime making the indios suffer.
  • Padre Damaso was an old curate at San Diego, he became close friends with Kapitan Tiago and Don Rafael but later sabotaged Don Rafael and said false things about him to put him in prison.
  • He is secretly the biological father of Maria Clara without Kapitan Tiago and Maria Clara knowing.
Pilosopo Tasio.
  • Born to a rich woman, Tasio grew up a talented young man, becoming a student of philosophy. However, as he demonstrated a great amount of skill, his pious mother asked him to either become a priest or abandon his studies. Having fallen in love with a woman, Tasio chose the latter option and married.
  • In less than a year, both Tasio's mother and his wife died; in response, he immersed himself in his studies, purchasing books and neglecting his estate until he became poor. For this, he was dismissed as a lunatic by the townspeople of San Diego. He also, at some point, gained the acquaintance of his neighbor Sisa and her two sons Basilio and Crispin.
Crispin
  • Born in San Diego to a woman called Sisa. He also has an older brother called Basilio.
  • Crispin was a young boy living in San Diego and one of the sacristans of its church alongside his brother Basilio.
  • After being accused of stealing from the church's coffers, Crispin was punished by the head sacristan and Padre Salvi.
Basilio.
  • Basilio is the son of Sisa and Pedro, and the older brother of Crispin. He is the only remaining member of his family due to the turn of events thirteen years ago. His brother was tortured until death by the Head Sacristan, which caused his mother to go crazy.
  • At a young age, Basilio demonstrated that he was mature and grown-up like, he was aware of his father's abuse towards his mother and recognizing the need to find his family.
